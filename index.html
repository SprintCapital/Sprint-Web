Who are we?

We are SPRINT, a financial services startup building a platform to make vehicle ownership accessible for gig workers with a 0% down payment on electric vehicles, a more inclusive alternative to traditional credibility checks that ensures an individual's credit history no longer serves as the main criteria for financing approval, and the chance to get behind the wheels of your own car and start earning in less than a week.
Have you ever dreamed of a life free from financial stress? Well, with SPRINT, you can #Ownit.
