Who are we?

SPRINT is a financial services startup making vehicle ownership accessible for gig workers with 0% down payments on electric vehicles, a more inclusive alternative to traditional credibility checks that ensures an individual's credit history no longer serves as a criteria for financing approval, and the chance to get behind the wheels of your own car and start earning in less than a week. Have you ever dreamed of a life free from financial stress? Well, with SPRINT, you can #OwnIt.

What Sets Us Apart

Vision

At SPRINT, we dream of a future where any individual and their family can afford life's basic necessities, regardless of their financial situation.

Philosophy

We firmly believe that financial well-being is a "fundamental right", not a privilege, so "No one should have to settle for less than they deserve".

Mission

We have set out to build the ultimate financing platform to provide our client network with access to services they won't be able to get elsewhere because they've been told countless times that they are not qualified to receive them.

Equal Opportunities

Driven by financial inclusion and the need for positive change, we strive to create equal opportunities for all

Empowering Women

With a vision for progress, we aim to maintain a 3:2 female-to-male driver ratio while empowering women with new opportunities.

Go Green with SPRINT

At SPRINT, sustainability matters. We're dedicated to a greener tomorrow, reducing CO2 emissions by exclusively using electric fleets.
